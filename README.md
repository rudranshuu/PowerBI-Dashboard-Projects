Netflix & Amazon Content Analysis
Analyzes content distribution by genre, rating, and country
Includes KPIs like total movies, genres, and directors
Visuals: line chart, bar chart, donut chart, map

Key Insights:
🔹 Insight 1: Content Growth

Content has increased significantly after 2010, indicating rapid expansion of streaming platforms.

🔹 Insight 2: Content Type Distribution

Movies make up the majority of content, while TV shows represent a smaller portion.

🔹 Insight 3: Genre Dominance

Drama is the most dominant genre across both platforms, followed by comedy and mixed genres.

🔹 Insight 4: Rating Distribution

Most content falls under mature and teen categories, suggesting a focus on adult and young audiences.

🔹 Insight 5: Geographic Distribution

Content is concentrated in a few countries, indicating that production is dominated by specific regions.

🔹 Insight 6: Platform Trend (if comparison visible)

Both platforms show similar growth trends, but the scale and distribution of content vary slightly
